# Google Cloud landing page

Bespoke single-page site for the Google Cloud partner conversation at eMerge Americas 2026. Built to feel native to cloud.google.com so a Google Cloud AE, partner-development lead, or ISV-program rep glancing at Matt's phone at the booth reads it as their own marketing surface with Eduba's content inside.

Intended URL path: /for/google-cloud

## What the page does

Opens with the section 7 headline verbatim and the hook from the fit analysis. Carries the 60 / 30 / 10 frame as a three-card visual layer. Lands the Correlation One case study with Pacific Life and Colgate-Palmolive as the peer pattern Google Cloud field teams already ask for. Surfaces ICM as the methodology substrate for ADK and Vertex AI governance, with the repo linked. Includes the required NLP Logix credibility line, the KPMG Big Four framing, the Ethics Engine bio with both arXiv and AuditEngine repo as live anchors. Closes with a Calendly CTA for Matt Creamer.

## Design

Google Sans via Roboto fallback. Google Material four-color palette with blue as the dominant brand color, green and yellow as accents, red in semantic territory only. Pill-shaped primary CTAs at 28px radius, 12px card radii, Material-grade shadows. Single-color soft radial gradients in the hero. No multi-hue chromatic gradients, no Lottie, no consumer-Google rainbow cues.
